Privacy Policy

Spice Rack Index is an offline-first app. All data you enter — including name, purchaseDate, shelfLocation — is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage (Application Support directory). We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs in this app, and it makes no network requests.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment information.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.
